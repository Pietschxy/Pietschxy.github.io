PC Games

Grand Theft Auto 5

Grand Theft Auto is an Open-World-Computer game, which is developed by the Scottish Studio Rockstar Games North. It was published on the 17th of September 2013. The game takes place in a fictional city called Los Santos, which is a reference to the real city Los Angeles. The game is separated into two parts. First you are able to play a fully developed Story-Mode, where you play the Story of three Bandits, who try to survive in our modern World. Each oft he is unique and comes with a special ability. During your gameplay you encounter a lot of problems. The story writing is great and you can have a lot of fun, if you are good at playing video games. Second is an Online-Mode. It isn’t finished meaning you get some updates with new features or new guns, vehicles, etc. every now and then. You start by customizing your character and after that you get thrown into a world full of brutality and sport cars. The game has a fully developed money system. You need money to upgrade your vehicles guns, etc. You can get money by grinding or simply paying with real money.

Overwatch

Overwatch is a multiplayer-ego-shooter game which is based on a fictional world where robots, humans, cyborgs, and genetically modified animals fight for peace or destruction of the world. It was made by Blizzard Entertainment Studios which are famous for games like World of Warcraft or StarCraft. It was published on the 24th of May 2016 and since then it has always been a very enjoyable game. Every round starts with a hero-selection-screen. The heroes are separated into classes. There are Tanks who have a lot of health but are slow and don’t do a lot of damage and they often have a shield ability with that they can protect their team. Then there are Damage-Dealers (DD) who do a lot of damage and have unique abilities so they can fight either in the air or are fast on the ground. And last but not least Healers! They are there for… healing of course. Their abilities heal other players and they even do a lot of damage. Every team consists of 6 players and most of the time there are 2 tanks, 2 damage dealers and 2 healers. Each one of them is important for the success of the mission and communication is the most important thing. the game even has its own E-Sport tournament.

Minecraft

Minecraft is a Sandbox-Computer game which was developed by the Swedish programmer Markus “Notch” Persson and his company Mojang. Minecraft had its first appearance on 17th May 2009 and since then over 125 million players enjoy the beautiful open world. With 235 million examples sold it is the most sold video game ever. The world inside the game is completely build of cubes and it sometimes breaks the laws of physics. But that is okay because when we want to play computer games we want to forget the real world. The world inside the game is very lively and you have several ways to start your journey. If you are lucky you even spawn beside a village and get some starter loot. But if you want to equip yourself like a badass, you have to go mining. Under the surface of the earth you can find lots of ores and resources which are useful on your journey. Eventually you discover some myths about the world and even encounter legendary creatures. But the best thing, you can play the survival mode with your friends. For those who don’t like farming there are several online servers where you can meet up or do some PVP.

Console games

Super Mario Odyssey

In this game you play as the well-known game character Mario. In this latest published game of the famous Super Mario franchise you play as Mario who is trying to save Rosalina! Just a joke it is of course Peach again… But this time you don’t travel alone. you get a companion called Cappy. He helps you on your journey because Bowser kidnapped his little sister. But Cappy isn’t only there as an NPC, in fact he has a very special ability which can helps you on your journey. You can throw him at an animal, a monster or even Bowser himself and then you can play as them. Together with your new companion Cappy you start a journey where you chase Bowser through 15 places to get Peach and Cappy’s little sister back.

Halo the Master Chief Collection

Halo was developed by 343 Industries and it was released on November 11th 2014 for Xbox one and on December 3rd 2019 for computer. Two days after its release on Steam it has already sold over 2 million copies.
This game is a summary of 6 six separated Halo games, which are called Halo: Combat Evolved Anniversary, Halo 2, Halo 3, Halo 3: ODST, Halo: Reach and last but not least Halo 4. All of these games were released on earlier Xbox platforms but for this special edition everyone received a graphical upgrade. The Edition was released because of the 10th Anniversary of the game and it received a good rating among the critics.
All of these games tell us a story about the fight between the UNSC (United Nations Space Command) and the Alliance. Easier said a fight between humans and aliens. These games are set in the future where humanity can already travel through space. But in this story the aliens are the superior race and the only opportunity for the humans to win are the so-called Spartans. Spartans are modified humans. They are faster, stronger and generally taller than normal humans. Every spartan has a unique Armor which can hardly be destroyed. You play as a spartan and have to kill aliens and discover a lot of secrets in the universe.

Ghost of Tsushmia

Ghost of Tsushima was developed by Sucker Punch Productions and it was published by Sony interactive Entertainment on 17th July 2020 for PlayStation 4.
The game is set during the first Mongol-Invasion of Japan in 1274 on the Island Tsushima. You play as a fictional Samurai called Jin Sakai. Your goal is to prevent the Mongols of going to the Japanese main island. But to fulfil your destiny you have to fight in a different way as the codex of the samurai tells you to. Together with your friends who you find on your way to kill the Mongol Leader Kothun Kahn you have to face different situations and most of them are very dangerous.

Mobile Games

Clash Royal

Clash royal is a free to play real time strategy game which was developed and published by Supercell. this game combines elements of collectible card games, tower defence and multiplayer online battle arena. It was released on March 2nd 2016 and it reached 1 billion revenue in less than a year.
In this game you can collect a lot of different cards, which can help you win against your enemies. Every card is unique and they are separated into different rarities. There is common, rare, epic, legendary and champions. Every card cost so called elixir and you can choose six of them to be on your team. when you start an online game, you have 5 elixir but with time it can rise up to a maximum of 10 elixir. If you put a card on the battlefield you lose the amount of elixir that the card cost. For every win you get trophies and one chest. The trophies help you to find players who have the same skill as you in the game. Players with high trophies only fight people with around the same number of trophies same goes for every number of trophies.

Pokémon Go

Pokémon Go was developed by Niantic for IOS and Android. It was published by Nintendo for some countries during July and for Korea in January.
It is a location-based game, which means your position gets tracked by a satellite. But to play this game it is necessary because you have to move around in the real world in order to find Pokémon because the spawn all around the globe. If you find one you have to get clos and then you can catch it. You catch them by throwing a Poke-Ball and if you are successful it is yours. Every Pokémon has different stats and soma are declared rarer than others but in order to win fights against other trainers you have to be the very best.
